About Me

I am Edward Opich, an aspiring video game developer, and amateur artist and musician!

I've been passionate about all things video game from a very early age, and started on my never-ending quest to learn how to make them at the age of 12. I started off with a super simple don't-touch-the-walls maze game, and from there came to learn how to make some of the stuff you see here!

My biggest interest is programming; I've always loved being able to make something tick. Whether it be gameplay programming, engine development, graphics programming, sound programming, or anything else, I can promise you that if I don't already know all about it, I'm at least interested in it and eager to learn more about it!

I also have an interest in music and art; I play the bass guitar, and have done some MIDI arrangements in my spare time. I've also made some chiptunes using trackers like Famitracker and Deflemask. As for art, I dabble in pixel art, 3D modeling, and have been trying to learn how to make more appealing animations and characters.

If what I have here isn't enough to strike your fancy, have no fear; I'm just getting started. I'll keep adding more and more content to this site as my list of projects grow! But I think I've said enough. Take a look around for yourself!
