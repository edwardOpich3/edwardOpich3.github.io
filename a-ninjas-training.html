A Ninja's Training

A Ninja's Training is the earliest game I have on this site to date, made when I was 16, back in 2013, for a high-school class. There are some things I'm proud of in this game, while there are others that don't quite elicit the same feelings. Regardless of how good it actually is though, it was certainly a learning experience for me!

The game was created over the course of about a month using Game Maker 8.1. It was a solo project, with almost everything created by myself. The SFX were gathered from free online resources. The instruments for the MIDI soundtrack are the Windows default.
