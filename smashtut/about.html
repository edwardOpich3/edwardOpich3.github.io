About

I chose to make this site because I am very passionate about Smash Bros! I've been playing since around 2001, and figured that it would be pretty cool to write a tutorial on learning to play at a higher level, or at least as high as I am.

The information from this site was pulled directly out of my own experience.

The images on the site are from Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS, by Nintendo.
