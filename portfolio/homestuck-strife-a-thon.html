Homestuck Strife-a-thon

Homestuck Strife-a-thon is a fangame for the webcomic Homestuck, currently developed exclusively by me. When it's progressed to a more presentable state, I intend on looking for a team to really make it shine. It is heavily influenced by Super Smash Bros, with influences from Street Fighter, Killer Instinct, and various other games.

It is developed using the Allegro graphics library with C++. Graphics were made in GIMP.

Homestuck Strife-a-thon is still a work in progress, and isn't quite ready to be played. Expect more to come here as the game progresses!
