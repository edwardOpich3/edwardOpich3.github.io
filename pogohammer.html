Pogohammer

A 3D Model of the Pogohammer, one of the weapons of choice for John Egbert, of Homestuck fame. The model was made as an assignment for a class, and is my first serious attempt at creating a 3D model.

The pogohammer was modeled in Maya, with textures created using GIMP, Mudbox for the normal map, and mental-ray for the ambient occlusion.
