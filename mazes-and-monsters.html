Mazes & Monsters

You've stumbled upon an ancient, towering structure filled to the brim with ultra-rare playing cards for one of your favorite games! But the tower is also crawling with monsters from the game who would love a good snack! Dare to grab as many cards as you can while fending off the monsters and keeping yourself in one piece!

Mazes and Monsters was my second big attempt at designing and developing a game, back in 2014. I created the game in my free time, and solo; all assets and programming were done by me. The game is a Pac-man clone, with a bit of creative liberty taken, as well as sporting a Sega Master System inspired aesthetic in its artwork, music, and SFX.

The game is mostly finished, however I ended up moving on to bigger and better things before I implemented difficulty increases and other features.

The game was programmed using the Allegro graphics library with C++. Artwork was made in GIMP. The music and SFX was made using the Deflemask tracker.
